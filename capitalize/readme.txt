In this exercise, we want to take 10 names of people whose letters are irregularly in small
and capital letters as input, and standardize it, the meaning of standardization is to 
capitalize the first letter.

for example:

input:

BaHram
MaHnaZ
hooman
FaribORZ
barAN
HedieH
ALI
EZATOLLAH
MOHAMADALI
JAMSHID

output:

Bahram
Mahnaz
Hooman
Fariborz
Baran
Hedieh
Ali
Ezatollah
Mohamadali
Jamshid
